### KPIs Used

- Total Customers
- Average Sales per Order

### Visuals Included
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Segment
- Sales Trend by Year
- Sales by Sub-Category

### Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest revenue
- Consumer segment dominates overall sales contribution
- Business shows steady year-over-year growth
- Certain sub-categories contribute low sales despite high product counts

### Recommendation
Focus on expanding high-performing categories such as Technology,
while reviewing pricing or strategy for lower-performing sub-categories
to improve overall profitability.
